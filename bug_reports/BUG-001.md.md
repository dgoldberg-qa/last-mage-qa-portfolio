# Spell is selected automatically when releasing a finger after level up

**Severity:** Major

**Priority:** High

**Environment:**

**Device:** Samsung Galaxy A16

**OS:*** Android 15

**Preconditions:**

The game is installed.
A dungeon run is in progress.
The character is close  to leveling up.

**Steps to reproduce:**

1. Start a dungeon run.
2.  Keep the finger pressed on the movement area while fighting.
3. Gain enough experience to trigger the level-up screen.
4. Keep the finger on the screen while the spell selection window appears.
5. Lift the finger from an area occupied by one of the spell cards.

**Expected result:**

Releasing the finger should not select a spell automatically.
A spell should only be selected after an intentional tap on a spell card.

**Actual result:**

The spell located under the finger is selected automatically when the finger is lifted.

**Frequency:**

Always.

**Workaround:**

Move the finger to an empty area of the screen before lifting it.

**Evidence**

No screenshot available.
The issue is related to touch interaction and is best demonstrated by screen recording.
