# Game freezes for 1-3 seconds after starting a dungeon

**Severity:** Major

**Priority:** High

**Environment:**

**Device:** Samsung Galaxy A16
**OS:** Android 15

**Preconditions:**

The game is installed.
At least one dungeon is  unlocked.

**Steps to reproduce:**

1. Launch the game.
2. Open the dungeon selection screen.
3. Select any available dungeon.
4. Tap Start.

**Expected result:**

The dungeon loads smoothly.
Gameplay starts without noticeable freezes.

**Actual result:**

The  game freezes for approximately 1-3 seconds immediately after the dungeon starts.
During the freeze, FPS drops to approximately 1.
After the freeze, gameplay continues normally.

**Frequency:**

Always. Reproduced in every available dungeon.

**Workaround:**

None

**Evidence:**

No screenshot available.
The issue is performance-related and cannot be adequately demonstrated with a static image.
