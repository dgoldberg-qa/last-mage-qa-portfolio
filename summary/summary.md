## Main findings 

Two reproducible functional  issues were identified:

1. Automatic spell selection caused by touch input after leveling up.
2. Consistent game freeze for 1-3 seconds when starting a dungeon.

## Improvement suggestions

Several usability improvements were proposed, related to:

- Inventory discoverability
- UI layout
- Accessibility
- Combat flow
- Pause behavior

## Questions

Some gameplay mechanics could not be fully verified  due to the lack of documented expected behavior.

Questions were raised regarding:

- Item fusion conditions
- Progress saving behavior


## Overall assessment

The tested build is generally stable anf all core gameplay  systems are functional
Two reproducible bugs affecting gameplay and performance were identified.
Several usability improvements were also suggested to improve accessibility, interface clarity, and overall player experience.
Some gameplay mechanics require clarification from the developer before they can be classified as intended behavior or defects.

## Recommendations

Recommended priorities for the next build:

Fix the spell selection issue.
Investigate the dungeon loading freeze.
Clarify the item fusion mechanic
Improve discoverability of existing gameplay features.
